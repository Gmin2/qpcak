# Billing and invoices

We bill monthly or annually. Annual plans are discounted by about 20 percent compared to paying monthly.

Manage your plan under **Settings → Billing**. You can upgrade at any time and the change is prorated. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.

Invoices are emailed to the billing contact and are also available for download in the billing portal. We accept major credit cards and, on annual plans, payment by bank transfer.
