# Configuring single sign-on (SSO)

Acme supports SSO via SAML 2.0 and OpenID Connect. SSO is available on the Business and Enterprise plans.

To enable SAML SSO, go to **Settings → Security → Single Sign-On**, upload your identity provider metadata, and map the email and name attributes. You can enforce SSO so members must sign in through your identity provider.

We support Okta, Microsoft Entra ID, Google Workspace, and any standards-compliant provider. SCIM user provisioning is available on Enterprise.
