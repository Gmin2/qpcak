# Shipping and delivery

We ship worldwide. Standard delivery takes 3 to 5 business days within the country and 7 to 14 business days internationally. Express delivery is available at checkout.

Once your order ships, you receive a tracking number by email. You can also find tracking under **Orders** in your account.

Shipping is free on orders over 50 dollars. Customs duties for international orders are the responsibility of the recipient.
