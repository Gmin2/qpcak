# Resetting your password

If you forgot your password, click **Sign in** and then choose **Forgot password**. Enter the email address on your account and we will send a reset link.

The reset link is valid for 30 minutes. If it expires, request a new one. For security, old links stop working as soon as a newer link is requested.

If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder and make sure you entered the same email you used to sign up. Password reset emails are sent from no-reply@acme.example.
